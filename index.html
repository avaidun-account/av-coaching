Questions, answered.

Do I need a gym membership?

Not necessarily. I build your program around the equipment you have — full gym, home setup, or minimal gear. We'll make it work.

How does online coaching work?

You'll get your plan through a coaching app, message me anytime with questions, and we'll do weekly check-ins to review progress and adjust.

I'm a total beginner — is this for me?

Absolutely. A big part of my job is meeting you where you are and teaching you the fundamentals so you build confidence and momentum.

What if I have an injury or limitation?

We'll work around it. Programming is fully customized, and I'll always prioritize training in a way that's safe for you.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Plans are flexible. The Transformation package is a 3-month block, while 1:1 Coaching is month-to-month.
